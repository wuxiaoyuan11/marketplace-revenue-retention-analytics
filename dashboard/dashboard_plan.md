# Dashboard Plan

## Dashboard 1: Executive Overview

Purpose: give recruiters and interviewers a quick business view.

KPIs:

- GMV
- Orders
- Customers
- AOV
- Delivered order rate
- Average review score
- Average delivery days

Charts:

- Monthly GMV and order trend
- GMV by product category
- GMV by customer state
- Review score distribution
- Delivery delay vs review score

## Dashboard 2: Customer Retention and RFM

Purpose: show user behavior analytics ability.

Charts:

- Cohort retention heatmap
- Customer segment distribution
- GMV contribution by RFM segment
- Average order value by RFM segment

## Dashboard 3: Product and Operations Insights

Purpose: connect business performance to operational levers.

Charts:

- Top product categories by GMV
- Low-rated categories with high revenue impact
- Delivery time by state
- Review score by delivery delay bucket
- Payment type mix

## Recommended Tools

Use one of:

- Tableau Public
- Power BI
- Looker Studio

For internship applications, Tableau Public is a strong choice because you can share a public dashboard link on your resume.

See the detailed BI build guide:

```text
dashboard/tableau_powerbi_build_guide.md
```

BI-ready CSV exports are saved in:

```text
dashboard/bi_export/
```
